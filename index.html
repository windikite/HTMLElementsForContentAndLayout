The Dragonsong's Reprise (Ultimate) Guide

Death of the Heavens

Starting positions

  1. Relative north is whatever cardinal direction Grinnaux jumps to. For this we will assume A is relative north.
  2. Each player takes their position in a line like so, 1 through 8, north to south.
  3. Watch your character or your debuff bar to see if you receive a doom debuff.
  4. All players should have their cameras pointed in a regular direction for this. We will assume north to make it easy.
  5. All doom players step right.
  6. All non-doom players step left.
  7. This is important because it makes it much easier to count from north to south.
  8. Doom debuffs 1-3 go right, non-doom debuffs 1-3 go left.
  9. Doom and non-doom debuff #4 go across to the other side, closer to the middle.
  10. All players return to middle except the 4th of each debuff.
  11. The 4th debuffs linger farther out, reliably baiting part of the next mechanic on them.
  12. The doom players spread along the north of the inner circle.
  13. The non-doom players spread along the south of the inner circle.
